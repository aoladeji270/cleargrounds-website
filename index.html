About ClearGrounds

Built on Honesty.
Driven by Excellence.

At ClearGrounds, we believe every property deserves a fresh start. Based in Chafford Hundred and proudly serving Grays, Thurrock, Essex, and the surrounding areas, we provide reliable, high-quality end of tenancy cleaning, clearance, landscaping, and garden transformation services for homeowners, landlords, tenants, and businesses.

Founded by a team of friends with a shared passion for hard work, excellent service, and a clear passion for home aesthetics, built on simple values: professionalism, honesty, attention to detail, and treating every property as if it were our own.

Whether you're preparing a property for new tenants, clearing out an unused loft, transforming an overgrown garden, or giving your outdoor space a complete makeover, we're committed to delivering results that exceed expectations.

We take the time to understand your needs, provide clear communication from start to finish, and ensure every job is completed to the highest standard. Our mission is simple: to help our customers create cleaner, more attractive, and more functional spaces, with a friendly, dependable service they can trust.
